## What our best customers look like

- Mid-market vertical-SaaS companies (200-800 employees) that already run a
  modern data stack and are expanding into AI features. They buy because they
  want to ship AI without staffing a research team.
- Industrial automation firms modernizing legacy tooling — they value
  grounded, auditable outputs over flashy demos.

## Signals that correlated with closed-won

- A public AI/automation hire or product announcement in the last 6 months.
- An existing data/ML function (even a small one) — they understand the value.
- Mid-size: large enough to have budget, small enough to avoid a 12-month
  procurement cycle.

## Anti-patterns (lost or churned)

- Sub-50-employee companies — no budget, founder still making every call.
- Heavily regulated enterprises locked into an incumbent suite.
